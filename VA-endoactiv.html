How it works?

The inner ball roller machine is a non-invasive mechanical compression micro-vibration + infrared treatment. The principle is to generate compression micro-vibration by rolling the silicone ball along the 360° rotation of the roller. As the ball rotates and exerts pressure on the skin, it produces a “pulsation compression” effect, which realizes the reciprocating motion of continuous pushing, pulling and kneading, and the tissue will experience some pressure. And lifting action, will not squeeze or damage the skin, its principle is to release infrared rays while pressure is applied to the tissues to stretch the cells to naturally and deeply stimulate cell activity, blood flow and oxygenation, and the fat deposits are pressurized and thus loosened for final decomposition.
